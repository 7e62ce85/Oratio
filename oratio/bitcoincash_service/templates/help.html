What is Bitcoin Cash
    Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a branch of Bitcoin that retains the original goals of the creator(s) of Bitcoin. It is fast, cheap and can be anonymous to use. It is an open source decentalized payment system that no one controls and to summarize has no inflation. [The Bitcoin Core (BTC) you may hear about in the news was hijacked and sabotaged to become controlled, slow and expensive to use by the bank funded Blockstream company.]
